The Story

Centuries ago, the mystical Soulforge shattered, breaking the balance between magic and the world. Powerful fragments scattered across floating kingdoms, awakening monsters and forgotten guardians. As the last Soulforger, your mission is to recover the fragments, restore the Soulforge, and save the realm from falling into darkness.
